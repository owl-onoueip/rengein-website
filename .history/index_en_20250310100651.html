About Our Temple

Renge-in Temple is a Shingon Buddhist temple with a rich history dating back centuries. Our temple offers a peaceful sanctuary where visitors can experience traditional Buddhist practices and find spiritual guidance.

Founded in the tradition of Shingon Buddhism, our temple serves as a center for spiritual practice, cultural preservation, and community service. We welcome visitors from all backgrounds to experience the beauty and wisdom of Japanese Buddhist traditions.

The name "Renge-in" refers to the lotus flower (renge), which is a symbol of purity and enlightenment in Buddhism. Just as the lotus rises from the mud to bloom in beauty, we believe in the potential for all beings to attain enlightenment.

Temple Services

We offer various religious services and spiritual practices for both temple members and visitors.

Buddhist Ceremonies

Traditional ceremonies including memorial services, blessing rituals, and seasonal observances.

Meditation Sessions

Regular meditation sessions guided by experienced practitioners, suitable for beginners and advanced meditators.

Cultural Experiences

Experience traditional Japanese cultural practices including calligraphy, tea ceremony, and Buddhist art.
