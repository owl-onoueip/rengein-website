Temple Services

Renge-in Temple offers a variety of religious services and ceremonies rooted in the traditions of Shingon Buddhism. Our services are designed to provide spiritual guidance, commemorate important life events, and honor ancestors.

Whether you are seeking personal spiritual growth, wishing to honor departed loved ones, or celebrating important milestones, our experienced priests are here to guide you through these sacred practices.

Memorial Services

Annual Memorial Services

Annual memorial services (nenki hōyō) are conducted to commemorate the anniversary of a loved one's passing. These services are traditionally held on the 1st, 3rd, 7th, 13th, 17th, 23rd, 27th, and 33rd anniversaries.

Duration: Approximately 45 minutes
Participants: Family members and close friends
Scheduling: Please contact us at least two weeks in advance

Obon Services

During the Obon festival in August, we conduct special memorial services to honor ancestors and departed loved ones. This is a time when the spirits of the deceased are believed to return to this world to visit their relatives.

Duration: Various services throughout the Obon period
Dates: August 13-16 annually
Special lantern ceremony on the evening of August 16

Segaki Service

The Segaki service is held to remember and honor those who have passed away without family to remember them. This compassionate ceremony helps ease the suffering of hungry ghosts and brings peace to wandering spirits.

Duration: Approximately 1 hour
Date: August 15 during Obon
Open to all temple visitors and community members

Blessing Ceremonies

Home Blessing

Our priests can perform blessing ceremonies for new homes or businesses to purify the space and invite positive energy. These ceremonies help establish a sacred and harmonious environment for your family or business.

Duration: Approximately 30-45 minutes
Location: At your home or business
Scheduling: Please contact us at least one week in advance

Personal Blessings

Personal blessing ceremonies can be performed for various life events such as birthdays, academic achievements, career advancements, or recovery from illness. These ceremonies invoke divine protection and positive energy.

Duration: Approximately 20-30 minutes
Location: At the temple or your location
Available daily by appointment

Amulet and Talisman Blessings

We offer blessed amulets and talismans (omamori) for various purposes such as health, safety, academic success, and business prosperity. Each amulet is blessed through a special ritual to imbue it with protective energy.

Available at the temple reception area
Available during temple opening hours
Special amulets available during New Year and other festivals

Meditation and Spiritual Practices

Meditation is a core practice in Shingon Buddhism, helping practitioners develop mindfulness, compassion, and insight. We offer regular meditation sessions for both beginners and experienced practitioners.

Weekly Meditation Schedule

Day Time Type Level Location
Monday 6:00 - 7:00 AM Zazen (Seated Meditation) All Levels Main Hall
Wednesday 6:00 - 7:30 PM Guided Meditation Beginners Meditation Hall
Saturday 9:00 - 11:00 AM Meditation & Dharma Talk All Levels Main Hall
Sunday 4:00 - 5:30 PM Ajikan Meditation Intermediate Meditation Hall

For beginners, we offer introductory meditation classes on the first Saturday of each month from 2:00 to 3:30 PM. These sessions include basic instruction on meditation posture, breathing techniques, and mindfulness practices.
