Renge-in Temple

日本語

Welcome to Renge-in Temple.

Our temple has been a place of spiritual comfort for the local community since ancient times.

We offer daily prayers and seasonal events to bring peace to your heart.

Please feel free to visit our temple and experience the tranquility of Japanese Buddhism.
