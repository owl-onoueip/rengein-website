About Our Temple蓮花院について

Renge-in Temple was established in the early Kamakura period (around 1190) and has a history of over 830 years.

Annual Events年間行事

Obon Festival

From August 13th to 16th, we hold memorial services for ancestors. Please visit with your family for spirit altar decorations and grave visits.

See more

Segaki Service

On August 15th, we hold a memorial service for the souls of the deceased who have no living relatives to remember them and pray for world peace.

See more

Monthly Memorial Service

On the 1st and 15th of each month, we hold memorial services for ancestors and pray for the health of your family.
